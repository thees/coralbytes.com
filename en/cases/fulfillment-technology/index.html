Bootstrapping a multi-tenant SaaS platform and tech organization from scratch.

A Series A e-commerce fulfillment startup had the domain expertise and funding — but no codebase, no cloud infrastructure, and no delivery processes. We acted as the founding engineering core, designing and building the platform end-to-end while simultaneously bootstrapping the tech organization.

Challenge

Domain Expertise, Missing Technical Foundation

The startup had the necessary domain expertise, solid funding, and a clear vision to build an enterprise SaaS platform. What they lacked was the technical foundation to execute fast: there was no codebase, no cloud infrastructure, and no delivery processes in place.

  • The "Blank Canvas" Challenge: Going from zero to a scalable enterprise platform requires getting foundational architecture decisions right the first time.
  • Complex Isolation Requirements: The platform needed to securely serve multiple clients, demanding a robust multi-tenant architecture.
  • Bootstrapping the Organization: The challenge wasn't just writing code, but simultaneously building the entire tech organization, culture, and operational processes from the ground up.

Approach

CTO Advisory & Foundational Engineering

Acting as the foundational engineering core, we shaped the technical basis end-to-end while serving as a strategic sparring partner for the founders and management.

Technical Execution

  • Cloud-Native & Serverless: Designed a highly scalable serverless architecture on Google Cloud (GCP) utilizing TypeScript, Node.js, and GraphQL.
  • Multi-Tenant Automation: Developed a fully automated deployment pipeline (GitHub Actions, Docker, Terraform) that provided each client with a securely isolated environment.
  • Interface Design: Iteratively improved backend interfaces to reduce communication overhead within the growing engineering team.

Aligning the Organization & Culture

  • Strategic CTO Advisory: We advised management on structuring the tech department and navigating crucial early-stage technical decisions.
  • Hands-On Culture & Ops: Established the initial monitoring, alerting, and on-call processes (ITSM) to transition from a prototype mindset to professional operations.
  • Team Building: Heavily drove the technical hiring process and actively mentored new engineers to build a sustainable, high-performing culture.

Outcome

Production-Ready and Built to Scale

In less than two years, the SaaS platform evolved from zero to full production readiness and successfully went live for the first test customers. We delivered a resilient technical backbone and handed over a fully functional engineering organization.
